Frequently Asked Questions

You might be wondering, what is intentionality and why should I care? I choose to do things every day, why do I need to be intentional about it?

But how intentional are you, really? Do you ever find yourself acting on impulse or on default settings?

If someone cuts you up in traffic, is your default reaction to get angry, beep the horn, maybe even utter a swear word or two? If your answer is yes, you’re not alone!

But what if your main intention for the day was to not get wound up about things outside of your control. Maybe then you would save yourself some stress and wasted energy.

In today’s world, it’s so easy to get distracted by technology. Do find yourself doom-scrolling, losing focus on what you’re supposed to be doing, checking for email, text messages or social media updates in every free minute you have? What if your intention for that day was to be present and not be so distracted? How would things be better for you by not letting your mind wander?

Setting yourself one intention for every day could be a first step in addressing old habits, carving out time to do something different or being less distracted overall. If you formalise one thing to do each day and check in at the end of the day if you’ve achieved it, you can form new habits and routines…. Start today and see how being intentional could be the beginning of something new.
